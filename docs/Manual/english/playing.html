PLAYING THE GAME

Cro-Mag Rally has many different play modes, and can have between 1 and 6 players playing. The game takes place in the Stone Age, Bronze Age, and Iron Age. You must complete an Age before you can play tracks on later Ages. So, when you first install the game, you will only be allowed to play the Stone Age. When the Stone Age Tournament has been won, then the Bronze Age will open up, plus, you will get new cars to drive. Completing the Bronze Age will likewise open up the Iron Age.
