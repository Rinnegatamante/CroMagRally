TOURNAMENT MODE

In order to unlock the advanced tracks in the game and drive new cars, you must first play and win each Age's Tournament. There are three Ages, and each has three tracks in which you must compete:


STONE AGE

Desert

Jungle

Glacier
BRONZE AGE

The Great Wall

Crete

Giza
IRON AGE

Medieval

Viking Village

Atlantis


To win a race in tournament mode, you must do two things:

1. Finish the race in 1st place (or at least 3rd place in Easy Mode).

2. Collect all 8 arrowheads.

In Tournament Mode, you are given 3 retries per Age. If you lose a race then you may try again as long as you have retries remaining.
