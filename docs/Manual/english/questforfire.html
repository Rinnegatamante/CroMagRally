QUEST FOR FIRE

This multi-player mode is like a traditional "Capture the Flag" game. There are two teams: the red team and the green team. It is best to play this game with an even number of players so that each team is the same size.

Each team starts the game at their home base. Next to their home base are the other team's 6 torches:


A Red Team player by the Red Base


Your goal is to go to the other team's base, pickup your torches, and bring them back to your home base. The red team's base has a red flag, and their torches have a reddish flame. The green team's base has a green flag, and their torches have a green flame. So, red team members need to find all of the red torches and take them to the red flag base. Vice versa for the green team.


A Red Team player carrying a Red Torch


You can pick up your opponent's torch and hide them anywhere on the terrain. To pickup a torch simply run into it. To drop a torch, press a Throw key. To cause another player to drop their torch you can either ram into them, or hit them with an exploding weapon. To deposit a torch at your base simply touch the base and the torch will automatically be deposited.

The first team to retrieve all of their torches wins.
